Data Deletion Instructions

Service: Sentience Support Bot
Organization: Sentience Biosensors Private Limited
Effective Date: 16 May 2026
Last Updated: 16 May 2026
This page explains how users can request deletion of data associated with Sentience Support Bot and related WhatsApp support interactions.

1. What Data Can Be Deleted

Upon valid request, we may delete chatbot-related data associated with your use of Sentience Support Bot, including customer support conversation records, chatbot interaction history, message metadata, and related support records that are not required to be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory reasons.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to:

Email: as2659208@gmail.com

Please use the subject line:

Data Deletion Request – Sentience Support Bot

In your request, include the following:

  1. Your full name
  2. Your WhatsApp phone number used to contact the service
  3. A short description of your request
  4. Any additional details that help us identify your records correctly

3. Verification of Identity

To protect user privacy and prevent unauthorized deletion requests, we may ask you to verify your identity or confirm ownership of the phone number associated with the chatbot interaction before processing your request.

4. Processing Timeline

We aim to review and process valid deletion requests within a reasonable time. In many cases, requests will be handled within 7 to 15 business days, depending on complexity and legal or operational requirements.

5. Exceptions

Certain information may be retained where required for:

  • Legal compliance
  • Fraud prevention and security monitoring
  • Dispute resolution
  • Internal recordkeeping where permitted by law
  • Technical backup and recovery systems for a limited time

6. Effect of Deletion

Once deletion is completed, relevant chatbot-related records associated with your request may be removed from active systems, except where retention is required as described above. Deleted data may not be recoverable.
