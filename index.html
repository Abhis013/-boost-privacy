Privacy Policy for BOOST

Last updated: November 2025

BOOST (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application BOOST.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, age, or gender (if you choose to provide it).
  • Health and Wellness Data: manually entered or received from connected devices (e.g., blood pressure, hemoglobin, glucose, steps, sleep data, meal logs, etc.).
  • Usage Information: app usage patterns, preferences, and interaction logs to improve performance.
  • Device Information: device model, operating system, and version for app optimization.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the wellness features of the app, including:

  • Displaying personalized health insights and trends.
  • Setting reminders for hydration, medication, or workouts.
  • Offering lifestyle recommendations.
  • Ensuring secure synchronization and backup of your data (if applicable).

3. Data Storage and Security

All sensitive data is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

BOOST does not sell, rent, or share your personal or health data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

BOOST may use third-party APIs or SDKs (e.g., Google Firebase, Spoonacular, EndlessMedical) solely to provide app functionality such as data syncing, analytics, and content delivery. These services comply with their own privacy policies.

5. Your Data Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us at the email below. You may also delete your account at any time within the app settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
