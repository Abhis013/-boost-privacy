1. Who We Are

Sentience Support Bot is a customer communication and support service operated by Sentience Biosensors Private Limited. It may be used to help users with product support, chatbot interactions, service requests, appointment-related communication, notifications, and other customer support activities.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our WhatsApp support service, we may collect and process the following information:

  • Your phone number associated with your WhatsApp account.
  • Your profile name as provided through WhatsApp.
  • Messages you send to us and messages we send to you.
  • Timestamps and technical message metadata related to delivery, status, and routing.
  • Support-related information you choose to share with us.
  • Any attachments, files, or media you send through the service, where applicable.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve our chatbot and support systems.
  • Record support conversations for service continuity and troubleshooting.
  • Provide product, service, and account-related assistance.
  • Maintain service security, prevent misuse, and investigate technical issues.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. How Your Information Is Stored

Information you send through our chatbot may be processed and stored on secure cloud-based systems and internal support systems used by Sentience Biosensors Private Limited.

We may store message records, support details, and related metadata for operational, security, compliance, and service improvement purposes.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in the following situations:

  • With service providers and cloud infrastructure providers that help us operate the service.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • To protect our rights, users, systems, or operations.
  • As part of a corporate transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, if applicable.

6. Data Retention
